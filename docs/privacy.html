WattFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

The short version

WattFlow collects no data. None.

Details

  • WattFlow reads battery status (voltage, current, level, temperature) from the Android system to display charging power. These readings stay on your device.
  • Charge history is stored in a local database on your device only. It is never uploaded anywhere. Uninstalling the app deletes it.
  • WattFlow has no ads, no analytics, and no tracking SDKs. Nothing about you or your device leaves your device.
  • The Google Play version uses Google Play Billing to process the optional Pro purchase. Payment is handled entirely by Google Play; WattFlow never sees your payment details. See Google's Privacy Policy.
